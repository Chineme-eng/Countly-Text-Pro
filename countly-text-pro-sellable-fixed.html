Writing reminders

A few practical reminders for cleaner copy.

Break dense thoughtsOne long sentence can often become two stronger ones.
Trim repeated termsIf one word appears too often, the copy can start to feel flat.
Write for the formatA script, essay, email, and sales page should not all read the same way.
